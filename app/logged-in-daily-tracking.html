Track your commute trips to InceptionU


For each day of the Commuter Challenge, enter every mode and corresponding distance that you traveled in kilometers. You can choose multiple modes per day each segment of your trip to and from work.

For any assistance with your data, email us at info@commuterchallenge.ca

If you worked from home, please enter the distance you would have traveled if you had gone to your workplace. If you are working from home due to COVID-19, enter your local walking or cycling trips to accumulate calories burned.
